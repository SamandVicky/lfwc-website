Get Connected

We are here for you!

At Lifeway, we make it easy to get and stay connected with others as you journey through life. We know the value of having friends who will encourage and support us. We have groups for couples, men, women, families, youth, children, and special interests.

Men's Ministry

The Men’s Ministry at LifeWay Family Worship Center is committed to training and equipping men to be spiritual leaders in their homes, church, and places of business, and to developing them in Christian living using the most effective means to impact the world, making a positive difference in this generation and generations of the future. The ministry seeks to accomplish this through Bible study, discipleship classes, leadership development, accountability groups, mentoring, retreats, mission trips and special events.

WoMen's Ministry

Women’s Ministries endeavors to create an atmosphere where women can realize spiritual maturity and fellowship, develop their leadership skills, and further their growth so they can be fully devoted followers of Christ and empowered to be godly influencers in their home, in the local church, in their community, and in the world.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry of LifeWay Family Worship Center is committed to the reality that young people are an important and integral part of our church community. Our youth program seeks to foster the maturing of faith in the youth of our church through fun, interactive, practical teachings.

Single's Ministry

Our Singles’ Ministry is designed to provide a ministry to single adults that fosters community and individual wholeness through a healthy, thriving relationship with God and each other. This ministry also provides opportunities to build and improve our walk and relationship with God.

Children's Ministry

Our Children’s Ministry is designed to partner with parents to help our children glorify God and grow to their full potential in Christ by teaching them biblical truths in a fun and creative way. LifeWay’s Children’s Ministry endeavors to help each child discover their God-given purpose and gifts. We affirm and encourage them to use their gifts and talents “now” by providing opportunities for them to serve in the classroom and in the body of Christ.
