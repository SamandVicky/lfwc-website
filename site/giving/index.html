Tithing

Discover God’s Faithfulness Through Tithing

Tithing is a biblical principle that encourages believers to give the first 10% of their income to God as an act of faith and gratitude. It is a way of honoring Him first in our finances and acknowledging that everything we have comes from Him. When we faithfully practice tithing, we open the door for God to work in our lives in powerful ways. Throughout Scripture, God promises to provide and care for those who trust Him with their giving. As we have seen in our church community, placing God first through tithing allows us to experience His provision, faithfulness, and blessings in our lives and in the lives of our families.

“Honor the Lord with your wealth, with the firstfruits of all your crops.”

Proverbs 3:9


Through tithing, we express our gratitude, strengthen our faith, and partner in the work God is doing through the church.


Give with Purpose

Your Generosity Can Transform Lives

God has lavished us with blessings we could never earn—His grace, mercy, and the ultimate gift of Jesus Christ, who gave His life for our salvation. This unmatched generosity calls us to respond with hearts that give freely and joyfully.


When we give beyond our tithe, we step into a greater partnership with God’s work, using our resources to make a tangible difference. Every offering, no matter the size, helps support ministries, outreach programs, and initiatives that bring hope, love, and practical help to those in need. Generosity is not just about money—it’s about reflecting God’s heart and sharing His love with the world.
